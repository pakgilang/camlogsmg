# Product Context: CAMLOG

## Purpose
PWA application to document and upload logistics data (PO numbers, GIT numbers, photos of delivery notes/SJ, koli, material) for SMG Logistics.

## Users
Warehouse operators, logistics staff, and PICs (GILANG, DODY, VALENT, NIKE, COMDIV).

## Register
Product (utility app, mobile-first, high efficiency).

## Tone & Ambient
- **Ambient Scene**: Logistics operators typing PO numbers and taking photos in a dynamic, sometimes dusty, varied-lighting warehouse. They use handheld mobile devices on the move.
- **Design Persona**: Robust, clean, high-contrast, professional. Excellent readability under bright sunlight or dim warehouse corners. Big clickable targets for thumbs.
- **Visual Theme**: "Navy & Neon Accent" (Deep slate/navy base with high-leverage neon teal/emerald status and accents).
